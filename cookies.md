# Cookie Policy

*Last updated: 17 June 2026*

This site does not set cookies for analytics, advertising, or personalisation. The only cookies present are strictly-necessary technical cookies set by **Cloudflare**, which acts as the CDN and security layer in front of this site.

## Cookies set by Cloudflare

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| `__cf_bm` | Bot management — distinguishes automated from human traffic. | Session (max 30 min) |
| `cf_clearance` | Records that a browser challenge was solved, preventing repeated challenges. | 30 min–24 h |

These cookies are set by Cloudflare, not by this site's operator. They are strictly necessary for the security and integrity of the service and do not require consent under the ePrivacy Directive as implemented in Italian law.

More information: [Cloudflare Cookie Policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/cookie-policy/).

## Managing cookies

You can delete or block cookies through your browser settings. Blocking `__cf_bm` or `cf_clearance` may cause access issues if Cloudflare triggers a security challenge.

- [Chrome](https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647)
- [Firefox](https://support.mozilla.org/kb/enhanced-tracking-protection-firefox-desktop)
- [Safari](https://support.apple.com/guide/safari/manage-cookies-sfri11471/mac)
